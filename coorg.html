Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Mysore junction

Mysore Junction is a major railway station in Mysore, Karnataka, India. It is one of the busiest railway stations in the state and serves as a gateway to the city of Mysore.

Coorg

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a picturesque hill station in the Western Ghats of India. It is famous for its lush coffee plantations, stunning landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Abbey Falls

Abbey Falls is a stunning waterfall located near Madikeri in Coorg. Surrounded by lush coffee plantations and dense forests, it is a popular tourist destination known for its scenic beauty and tranquility.

Raja Seat

Raja Seat is a popular viewpoint in Madikeri, Coorg, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys. It is especially famous for its stunning sunsets and is a must-visit for nature lovers.

Madikeri Fort

Madikeri Fort is a historic fort situated in the heart of Madikeri, Coorg. Originally built in the 17th century, the fort has witnessed several renovations and houses a palace, museum, and a chapel within its premises. It offers panoramic views of the town and is a significant landmark reflecting the region's rich history.

Day 2

Namdroling Monastery

Namdroling Monastery, also known as the Golden Temple, is a prominent Tibetan Buddhist monastery located in Bylakuppe, Coorg. It is one of the largest Tibetan settlements in India and is known for its stunning architecture and serene atmosphere.

Nisargadhama

Nisargadhama is a beautiful island located near Dubare in Coorg. It is a popular picnic spot known for its lush greenery, bamboo groves, and serene environment. Visitors can enjoy nature walks, elephant rides, and river rafting in the nearby Cauvery River.

Dubare Elephant Camp

Dubare Elephant Camp is a popular eco-tourism destination located near the Dubare River in Coorg. It is known for its elephant interactions, where visitors can learn about these majestic animals, go on elephant rides, and even participate in their bathing and feeding.

Day 3

Talakaveri temple

Talakaveri is the source of the river Kaveri, located in the Brahmagiri hill range in Coorg. The temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is situated at the site and is a popular pilgrimage destination. The area is known for its scenic beauty and is surrounded by lush greenery.

Gleolorna Coffee Plantation

Gleolorna Coffee Plantation is a renowned estate in Coorg, offering visitors a chance to explore lush coffee fields, learn about the coffee-making process, and enjoy the serene beauty of the Western Ghats. Guided tours provide insights into traditional coffee cultivation and processing, making it a must-visit for coffee enthusiasts.

Mandalpatti

Mandalpatti is a scenic viewpoint located near Madikeri in Coorg. It offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Mysore junction

Mysore Junction is a major railway station in Mysore, Karnataka, India. It is one of the busiest railway stations in the state and serves as a gateway to the city of Mysore.
