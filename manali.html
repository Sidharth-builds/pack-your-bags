Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Manali

Manali is a popular hill station located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is known for its stunning landscapes, adventure sports, and vibrant culture.

Old Manali

Old Manali is a quaint village located just a few kilometers from the main town of Manali. It is known for its picturesque landscapes, apple orchards, and a laid-back atmosphere that attracts backpackers and nature lovers.

Hidamba Temple

The Hidamba Temple, also known as the Hidimbi Devi Temple, is a famous temple dedicated to Hidimba Devi, the wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata. Surrounded by cedar forests, this ancient temple is known for its unique architecture and serene atmosphere.

Vashisht Temple

Vashisht Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to Sage Vashisht, known for its hot springs and stunning architecture. It offers a serene environment for meditation and spiritual practices.

Vashisht Hot Springs

Vashisht Hot Springs are natural hot springs located in the village of Vashisht, near Manali. These springs are believed to have medicinal properties and are a popular spot for tourists seeking relaxation and rejuvenation.

Jogini Falls

Jogini Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Vashisht village, just a short trek from Manali. Surrounded by lush greenery, it is a popular spot for picnics and photography.

Day 2

Old Manali

Old Manali is a charming village known for its scenic beauty, apple orchards, cozy cafes, and relaxed vibe. It's a favorite spot for backpackers and travelers seeking a peaceful retreat away from the bustle of the main town.

Solang Valley

Solang Valley is a picturesque valley located near Manali, known for its stunning landscapes and adventure sports. Visitors can enjoy activities like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing in the winter months.

Rohtang Pass

Rohtang Pass is a high mountain pass located on the Pir Panjal Range of the Himalayas, about 51 kilometers from Manali. It offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts.

Day 3

Old Manali

Old Manali is a charming village known for its scenic beauty, apple orchards, cozy cafes, and relaxed vibe. It's a favorite spot for backpackers and travelers seeking a peaceful retreat away from the bustle of the main town.

Naggar Castle

Naggar Castle is a historical castle located in the Kullu Valley, offering stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. It is a popular destination for tourists seeking to explore the rich history and culture of the region.

Jana Falls

Jana Falls is a picturesque waterfall located about 30 km from Manali, nestled amidst deodar trees and apple orchards. The falls are accessible via a scenic drive and a short trek, making it a favorite spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Visitors can enjoy the tranquil surroundings, take a refreshing dip in the cool waters, and savor traditional Himachali cuisine at local dhabas nearby.

Mall Road shopping

Mall Road is the main street in Manali, lined with shops, cafes, and restaurants. It's a great place to shop for local handicrafts, woollen clothes, and souvenirs. The vibrant atmosphere and stunning views of the surrounding mountains make it a must-visit destination.
